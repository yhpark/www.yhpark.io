Yeong Hoon Park

Hi, I'm Yeong Hoon Park, a full-stack developer and research engineer best known as the co-founder of Seoul Bus [1]. I currently live in Seoul, Korea, working full-time at Kakao Corp.

Throughout the years, I've been involved in a wide array of projects ranging from mobile/web applications to large-scale backend systems, among which most notable is KakaoTalk for Android. I recently joined ART (Advanced Recommendation Technologies) team at Kakao where we're building recommender systems that provide relevant content recommendations to tens of millions of users on Kakao's various mobile and web services.
